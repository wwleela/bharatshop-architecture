Features
What BharatShop OS does
◎
Zero-Entry Inventory
Gemini 1.5 Flash scans supplier bills and auto-updates stock. No typing. No errors.
◑
UPI Payments
NPCI deeplink UPI at checkout. Zero commission. Works with GPay, PhonePe, BHIM.
◐
Daily AI Briefing
Morning stock summary in Telugu, Hindi or English. Powered by Gemini.
●
Real-Time Dashboard
Live inventory with low-stock alerts. One-tap visibility of what's running out.
◔
Offline-First
Queue-and-sync for patchy connectivity. Designed for semi-urban India.
◕
Multilingual UI
Full Telugu, Hindi and English. Error messages in the owner's own language.
